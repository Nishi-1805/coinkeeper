About Day-to-Day Expenses

Welcome to Day-to-Day Expenses, your go-to application for managing your daily financial transactions. Below is a comprehensive list of features that our application offers:

Features

  • Currency Selection: Start by selecting your preferred currency using the search box on the main page.
  • User Accounts:
    • Existing User: Log in using your email ID and password.
    • New User: Register by filling out a form to create a new account.
    • Guest Account: Access the application without registration using sample credentials.
  • Forgot Password: If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link to receive a reset link via email.
  • Dashboard: Upon logging in, you will be directed to your dashboard, where you can manage your notes and transactions.
  • Notes Feature: Add, edit, and manage your notes. You can create as many notes as you want.
  • Pagination: Use the pagination feature to view your notes in a structured format. Simply click on the three horizontal lines located in the top right corner of the page. This will provide you with the option to enter a specific number, allowing you to display your desired quantity of notes per page for improved clarity and accessibility.
  • Premium Membership:
    • Purchase Premium: Click the diamond icon to buy a premium membership through Razorpay.
    • Leaderboard: Access the leaderboard feature to view transaction details of all users.
    • Monthly & Yearly data:Get access to adding monthly and yearly expenses data.
    • Download file:Get the download txt file feature on monthly expense page to get all your daily, monthly and yearly transactions.
  • Transaction Management:
    • Add Transactions: Record your income and expenses using the plus button.
    • Monthly and Yearly Pages: Add and view transactions on monthly and yearly pages.
    • Download Transactions: Download a text file containing all your daily, monthly, and yearly transactions.
